About Us

What we consider most important are the fans who look forward to our works. Those fans who cry, laugh, and have their hearts touched by the stories we bring to life.... Thinking of each and every face on the other side of the screen, we wish to create animation that makes them say to themselves, “I am glad I encountered this animation”, that makes them want to watch it over and over again.

As a studio, we strive to deliver animation that will remain in the hearts and minds of our fans, while challenging new forms of expression and experimenting with various genres without focusing on any particular style.

We take care of materials, schedule, budget, and quality control. To bring the production to completion, we mediate other departments while building a wide range of knowledge and experience.

Our Services

High Quality Animation

MAPPA is renowned for its exceptional animation quality and dynamic visuals, as seen in works like Attack on Titan: The Final Season, Jujutsu Kaisen, and Chainsaw Man.

Diverse Portfolio

The studio has a diverse range of projects, including action-packed series (Vinland Saga), emotionally driven dramas (Banana Fish), and unique artistic ventures (Yuri on Ice). .

Complex Stories

MAPPA often takes on anime adaptations with deep and intricate storylines. Its adaptations of manga with intense themes and complex character arcs have earned critical acclaim.
